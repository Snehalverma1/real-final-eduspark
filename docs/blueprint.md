# **App Name**: EduSpark

## Core Features:

- User Registration & Login: Allow students and instructors to securely create accounts and log in to the platform.
- Course Discovery: Enable students to browse and search for courses by subject, instructor, or popularity.
- Instructor Course Creation: Provide a simplified interface for instructors to upload video lectures, written content, and basic course details.
- Lecture Playback & Reading: Students can seamlessly consume course content, whether through integrated video playback or formatted text lessons.
- Student Progress Tracking: Allow students to mark lessons as complete and view their overall progress within each enrolled course.
- AI-powered Q&A Tool: An intelligent AI tool that provides instant answers to student questions related to course materials, offering explanations and clarifications.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color, #1C5ECB, a rich, inspiring blue, anchors the interface with a sense of wisdom and clarity, reflecting the core mission of learning and intellectual growth.
- A pristine background color, #E9EEF4, a very light blue-grey derived from the primary hue, provides an airy and focused canvas that enhances readability without distraction.
- The accent color, #1BB4D9, a vibrant and dynamic cyan-blue, is thoughtfully chosen to highlight key interactive elements and provide energetic focal points, subtly motivating user engagement.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) imparts a modern, tech-forward, and crisp aesthetic, suitable for titles and emphasized text.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) offers exceptional readability and neutrality, making it ideal for presenting extensive course content and ensuring a comfortable learning experience.
- Clean, geometric line icons are employed throughout, providing clear visual cues that reinforce the platform's focus on structured learning and intuitive navigation.
- A content-first layout strategy emphasizes clarity and ease of access to educational materials, utilizing generous whitespace to reduce cognitive load and enhance focus.
- Subtle and purposeful micro-animations are designed to guide user attention and provide immediate feedback on interactions, creating a seamless and engaging learning journey.
